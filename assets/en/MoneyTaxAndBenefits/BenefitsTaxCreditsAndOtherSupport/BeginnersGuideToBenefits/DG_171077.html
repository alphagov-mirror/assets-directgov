About the Benefits adviser and Jobseeker's Allowance online

The benefits adviser and Jobseeker's Allowance online are services provided by the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP), through the Directgov website.

Benefits adviser

The benefits adviser is an online questionnaire to help you find out if you are entitled to benefits and tax credits.

The results given are based only on your answers to the questions asked by the benefits adviser.

For a list of benefits and tax credits covered by the benefits adviser, please use the link below.

Jobseeker's Allowance online

All online claim forms are completely confidential and information is held securely.

The online claim form takes you 30 - 60 minutes to complete on Jobseeker's Allowance online. You can complete the claim in one go or you can save your claim and return to it later.

Until you have submitted your claim form, you have not made a claim to Jobseeker's Allowance.
