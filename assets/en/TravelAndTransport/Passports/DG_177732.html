Belfast Regional Passport Office

You have to visit a Regional Passport Office if you need a passport urgently and are applying in person using Fast Track or Premium services. You must make an appointment by calling the Identity and Passport Service Passport Adviceline on 0300 222 0000. The Adviceline is open between 8.00 am and 8.00 pm Monday to Friday and 9.00 am and 5.30 pm on weekends and public holidays.
