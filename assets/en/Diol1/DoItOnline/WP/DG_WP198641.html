State Pension online

Thank you for using the Government Gateway to apply for your State Pension.

What you need to do next depends on the message you received in Government Gateway. Please read the three options below to see which one applies to you.

1. Registering for State Pension online

If your registration was successful you'll be sent a user ID and Activation Code in the post within the next seven days.

If you're already registered with Government Gateway when you register for State Pension online, then you'll already have a user ID. You'll be sent an Activation Code in the post within the next seven days.

Please also make sure you remember the password you provided as part of registering as you will need this later.

Once you receive your Activation Code/User ID, return to the 'Claim your State Pension online' page on Directgov to start your claim.

2. Unable to log in, register or activate the service

If you had a problem during the registration, activation or login stages you should wait at least 12 hours before you try again.

3. If your details can't be confirmed online

If your details couldn't be confirmed online, please contact the Pension Service on:

  • 0800 731 7898 (8.00 am to 8.00 pm)
  • 0800 731 7339 textphone if you have speech or hearing difficulties

Welsh speaking customers living in Wales can call:

  • 0800 731 7936 (8.00 am to 8.00 pm)
  • 0800 731 7013 textphone if you have speech or hearing difficulties
