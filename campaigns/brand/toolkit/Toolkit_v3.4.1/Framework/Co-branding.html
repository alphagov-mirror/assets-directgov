Co-branding

You can add a logo for joint accreditation or co-branding of a tool or transaction. The 'Service provided by' module indicates which department or agency is responsible for the transaction or tool.

Logo placement

This module normally appears at the top of the fixed width right hand column.

In the absence of the right hand column it may appear in the left hand column.

The department/agency logo is placed between the "Service provided by" header and baseline with a margin of 40 pixels above and 45 pixels below the logo.

It is optional whether the logo is a clickable link to more information.
